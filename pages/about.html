PRIME MOVERS IN CONTEMPORARY APPLIED ART

It was founded in 1998 by Bob in the renaissance spirit, combining traditional and modern techniques to produce once-in-a-lifetime objects. At once highly specific and yet entirely universal, personally expressive and yet experimental, It has crafted a body of work that draws upon classical, popular and contemporary design and highly visual and sculptural art. This creates is heraldic and regal even in it's imagery. As sleek as the finished work can be, it is also instinctual and almost primal.
